About

Hello there. Thanks for visiting my portfolio website. I’m a web designer and developer who loves to discover new things and give my best. I am from Syria, where I graduated from the Institute of Computer Technology in Aleppo. I am also currently studying Informatics Technology Engineering at the Syrian Virtual University.

My passion for web design and development started when I got a grant from the Syrian Youth Gathering to access Coursera, an online learning platform. There, I learned a lot of skills and techniques that helped me create beautiful and functional websites. I also gained a lot of confidence and motivation to pursue my career goals.

I enjoy creating websites that are responsive. I also like to learn new things and solve problems using design thinking and coding skills. My dream is to work on projects that make a positive impact on people and society.

If you want to know more about me or my work, feel free to contact me or check out my portfolio. I would love to hear from you and collaborate with you on your next project.
